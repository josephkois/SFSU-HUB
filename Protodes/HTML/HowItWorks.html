Design Philosophy

Our design philosophy was to take a minimalist approach and create an application that is easy and quick to use, without the headache of not being able to find what you are looking for, or being put off by having too many options.

Safety & Security

Safety is a priority for all Gator Companions, users will not be able to see your contact information unless they are registered and logged in with an active sfsu email and an active student ID number.

It's Free to use

At Gator Companion, we believe students should have access to all these features without a subscription cost. Gator Companion is free to use for all users, whether registered or not.
